I'm May, and I've been cosplaying since 2010. I'm a cosplayer and photographer. I've been into anime ever since I was a kid (Sailor Moon and Pokemon), so I was bound to run into cosplay eventually. I found pictures of Kipi cosplaying Mio Akiyama, and wanted to try cosplay too! I bought a black dress and got my mom to alter it for me, and I bought a cheap Ebay wig. I wore my casual Mio to a local con, and had so much fun! Let's just say I've been addicted since then.

I wear a mix of both premade costumes and handmade ones. Miccostumes and Uwowo are both good brands, though I prefer Miccostumes because their sizing is bigger. I also make a lot of my costumes too! I usually make form fitting costumes and any of the really detailed ones. It's easier to buy a costume that does not need exact measurements to look good (yukata, uniforms).

I style most of my wigs. I like using Epic Cosplay Wigs, but I also buy from Arda Wigs or Ebay occasionally.
